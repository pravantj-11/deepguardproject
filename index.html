How It Works

Four layers of protection,
delivered in one blink.

Every session runs a full multi-modal pipeline — face, voice, liveness, and behavior — simultaneously, completing before your user finishes saying their name.

📡
Capture

Video and audio are pulled at 30fps directly from the browser or native SDK. Nothing is stored — all inference runs in-memory with end-to-end encryption.

🧠
Analyze

Three specialized models run in parallel: a face-forgery detector, a voice-clone classifier, and a liveness prober checking depth, blink rate, and micro-expressions.

⚖️
Score

Signal fusion via calibrated ensemble — all three modalities collapse into a single trust score (0–100) with granular breakdowns your team can audit anytime.

🔒
Decide

Pass, review, or block webhooks return in under 200ms. Native plugins for Jumio, Onfido, Stripe Identity, and any custom KYC pipeline via REST API.
